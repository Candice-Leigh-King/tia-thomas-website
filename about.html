Hello, I'm Tia, and I'm excited to welcome you to this blog.

If you're a fellow travel enthusiast planning your next adventure, you've come to the right place. My ultimate life goal is to explore every country on Earth, with Montenegro, Guatemala, and Egypt ranking among my favorite destinations, and Pakistan, Argentina, and Kazakhstan on my travel bucket list. My journey has been marked by unforgettable adventures, from volcano boarding in Nicaragua to exploring Egyptian pyramids and hiking Machu Picchu. But it hasn't all been smooth sailing; I've faced challenges like being stranded at a border and having my cards blocked in a foreign land. These experiences inspired me to create this blog to share insights and help you navigate your travels more smoothly.

My passion for travel began early, and in 2018, I embarked on my first solo backpacking adventure through Southeast Asia. It kindled my love for budget travel, which became a defining feature of my explorations. While battling a health scare, I embarked on a journey to Zambia for a raw adventure. There, I received a life-altering diagnosis of Crohn's Disease. This revelation reshaped my travel plans and my perspective on life.

Despite setbacks, I continued to explore Europe while focusing on managing my health. The advent of COVID-19 reminded me of the fleeting nature of travel privileges. I took the opportunity to plan a dream journey through Central and South America, which I undertook with my partner, Ben. After returning, I created TeeTravels on Instagram to connect with fellow travel enthusiasts. Eventually, I took the bold step of leaving my job to travel full-time, embarking on a journey across Europe and Canada, with a working visa adventure.

As we look ahead, 2024 promises exciting destinations, including Qatar, Pakistan, Australia, Japan, South Korea, Philippines, Vietnam, Laos, and Thailand. Follow my Instagram to stay updated on my daily adventures.
