Hello! I'm Tia, and I'm thrilled you've found this blog.

If you're here, chances are you share my travel obsession and are planning your next adventure. As a self-proclaimed travel addict, my ultimate life goal is to explore every country on Earth. Montenegro, Guatemala, and Egypt top my list of favorite destinations, while Pakistan, Argentina, and Kazakhstan are high on my bucket list. I've checked off incredible experiences like volcano boarding in Nicaragua, exploring Egyptian pyramids, and hiking Machu Picchu.

However, it hasn't all been smooth sailing. I've faced challenges, from being stranded at the Cambodia-Vietnam border to having my cards blocked in Indonesia. These experiences inspired me to create this blog to help you navigate your journeys more smoothly.

My passion for travel began early, with dreams of exploring the world. In 2018, I embarked on my first solo backpacking trip through Southeast Asia, discovering a newfound love for budget travel. After returning home, I couldn't shake the urge to plan my next adventure. I craved the excitement of solo travel and ventured to Zambia for a raw adventure. But a health scare led to a Crohn's Disease diagnosis, reshaping my travel plans and outlook on life.

Despite setbacks, I continued to explore Europe, emphasizing health management. As we all know, COVID-19 disrupted our lives, serving as a stark reminder of the impermanence of travel privileges. I seized the opportunity to plan my dream trip through Central and South America with my partner, Ben. Upon returning, I founded TeeTravels on Instagram, cultivating a community of travel enthusiasts. I left my job to travel full-time, journeying through Europe, Canada, and embarking on a working visa adventure.

The future remains uncertain, but 2024 promises exciting destinations, including Qatar, Pakistan, Australia, Japan, South Korea, Philippines, Vietnam, Laos, and Thailand. So follow my Instagram for daily adventures! Let me know if you'd like any further adjustments or have specific points you want to emphasize.
